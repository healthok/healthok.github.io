FAQ

1. How can I place an order ?
You can place orders by calling our toll free number 1800-3000-6588 or by also sending message via SMS, Whatsapp to 9794430208 or email to order@health-ok.in
2. Payment option?
We support Cash on delivery to keep it simple for you.
3. What is the delivery time ?
We will try to deliver the order as soon as possible. We guarantee delivery no later than end of next day.
Q4. What are the delivery or sample collection charges?
As a service provider we do not charge any additional fees for delivery of medicine or pickup of sample from home.
5. How can I cancel my order?
The order can be cancelled anytime by sending us an email, phone call, SMS or Whatsapp message.
6. What is your return policy and refund option?
We strive for 100% satisfaction and will accept returns if it is not used. Because of safety reasons we cannot accept used/opened packages.
7. How do I know the medicines delivered are authentic?
We have team of certified pharmacists/doctors who verify every single order you furnish to us. If a prescription is required, it will be verified too. We make sure you get what you asked for. Original and Genuine. We deliver products with proper bill, in case you have any issue, you may call our customer care.
8. Is there any discount?
We provide 10% discount on any recurring orders
9. What if my order does not get serviced?
We strive hard to provide timely service. If for any unfroseen conditions we are unable to meet the timelines we will refund the cost of medicine and also provide addition store credit to be used as discount for subsequent purchase.
10. How can I check my order status?
. We can provide automatic updates via email / SMS. You can always call our toll-free number and get status.
